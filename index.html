About this Converter

This tool converts between force constants (used in molecular dynamics simulations like GROMACS) and pressure values. The conversion factor used is 1.6605390671738, which converts kJ/mol/nm to MPa/nm².

Formula for Force Constant to Pressure: P = k × conversion_factor × (x × y)

Formula for Pressure to Force Constant: k = P / (x × y) / conversion_factor
